Working Bees

There is always something for eveyone - plenty to be done!

Keep it clean and green

Our main goal is keep our region clean and green free from pollution. So we are looking for volunteers who are keen to lend a helping hand whilst enjoy the great outdoors with a team of people, having fun and doing something that is important to us and our community. We are also on the lookout for those who are happy to contribute their skills in outdoor maintenance and other projects such as ensuring that the walking tracks are in good conditions. Able to do light repairing jobs if required. If you have any particular skills or preferences, please do let us know so we can find a perfect job for you.

2018 Te Peka Sounds Half Marathon Run

One of our main events at Te Peka is our yearly half marathon run

A fun day out for eveyone

Looking for an annual running event to keep fit and having fun at the same time? Want to add some challenge and variety to your exercise regime? Then we have the beach and coastal run options that you’re after. Our Beach Runs take you through the full length of the beautiful Te Peka Sounds Beach. Running on sand will surely get those legs working to see if it has got what it takes to be fit as a fiddle. Our native bush is full of fun and challenging tracks! Imagine being accompanied by the beauty of nature and satisfaction guranteed fresh air while you run!

Run at your own pace or be a supportive spectator

The course takes you around the beautiful Te Peka Sounds coastline and the ever changing tides and weather conditions make every race unique. What’s more, if you find yourself at the top of the leaderboard you’ll get your hands on some free trail running shoes, courtesy of Te Peka Sounds. So what are you waiting for? Go on, grab your running shoes and head to Northwardes on Sunday 28th of October for the 2018 Te Peka Half Marathon Run. Starting and finishing at the entrance of the 21 km Te Peka Sounds Wondering Track which is mainly flat but with a few hidden hills to enhanced your challenge!
