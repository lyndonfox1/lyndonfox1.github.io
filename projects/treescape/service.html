Crown lifting

The arborists from Oakwood Treecare and Consultancy can let light in by pruning the tree’s lower canopy, to raise the level of the branch line higher.

Crown reduction

Crown reduction lets in more light by reducing tree height and spread of the crown, while still retaining the shape of the crown.

Crown thinning

Crown thinning involves pruning out branches from the crown of the tree, while leaving an aesthetically pleasing height. This is particularly useful for Pohutukawa and other protected trees where tree topping is not an option – but branches under a certain diameter can be removed.

Tree pruning

Removing large branches can let light into your home and improve your garden space instantly.

Tree removal
